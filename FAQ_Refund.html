Q. What are the modes of refund available after cancellation?

The different modes of refund available are:

Payment mode: Cash on Delivery

1. UPI (Amount less than Rs 1000) - Refund will be processed to the UPI account for which UPI ID has to be shared

2. Gift Card (Amount less than Rs 10000) - Refund will be processed to Gift Card account

3. Bank account (IMPS/NEFT) - Refund will be processed to bank account for which bank details have to be shared Payment mode: Prepaid

4. Back to source - Refund will be processed to the payment mode(Debit/Credit card, NEFT, UPI) used to pay for the order2. Gift Card (Amount less than Rs 10000) - Refund will be processed to Gift Card account.
